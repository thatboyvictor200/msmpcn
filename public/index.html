Welcome to Mary Slessor Memorial Presbyterian Cathedral

At Mary Slessor Memorial Presbyterian Cathedral Our vision is to raise leaders, who are modeling Christ. Our church is dedicated to spreading the love and teachings of Christ. We believe in serving the community, fostering a Godly environment for all and Bringing People closer to GOD. This mission is centered around encouraging everyone, everywhere to recognize, maximize and utilize their God-given potentials for the Glory of GOD. We ister a biblically-based church health model which has an intentional emphasis to create the essential elements of a healthy, growing, biblically-based society

What Makes Us Unique

Daily Prayers

Life can get overwhelming — that’s why we pray every day. From quiet personal moments to uplifting group sessions, our daily prayers help us stay connected to God and refreshed in His presence. You’re always welcome to join in.

Continous Teaching

We believe in learning together, growing stronger in faith one step at a time. With Bible studies, interactive classes, and down-to-earth teachings, there’s always something new to discover — no matter where you are on your journey.

Set of Sermons

Every sermon is real, relevant, and rooted in God’s Word. Whether you’re tuning in online or sitting in the pews, you’ll hear messages that speak to your heart and help you live out your faith in everyday life.

Community Helpers

We don’t just talk about love — we live it. Through outreach, support programs, and caring hands, we’re here to lift up our community and make a difference where it truly counts. When one of us hurts, we all step in.
