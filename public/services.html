Welcome to Mary Slessor Memorial Presbyterian Cathedral

At Mary Slessor Memorial Presbyterian Cathedral Our vision is to raise leaders, who are modeling Christ. Our church is dedicated to spreading the love and teachings of Christ. We believe in serving the community, fostering a Godly environment for all and Bringing People closer to GOD. This mission is centered around encouraging everyone, everywhere to recognize, maximize and utilize their God-given potentials for the Glory of GOD. We Commentsister a biblically-based church health model which has an intentional emphasis to create the essential elements of a healthy, growing, biblically-based society
